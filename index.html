Hi, my name is Daniel Polk. I love building things that make people's lives better. From launching a charter school, to building a college ministry, to developing web applications, I've always focused on finding creative solutions to complex problems.


In completing my Full Stack Web Development Certification, through the Bootcamp at the University of Texas in Austin, I'm skilled with frontend and backend development requirements. I love working with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and jQuery to create dynamic frontend designs that are fast loading, user friendly, and mobile responsive. I also enjoy using tools like Node.js, Heroku, Ajax, API’s, mySQL, MongoDB, and others to help the backend communicate with the frontend.


Learning all of these languages has helped me grow in my project management skills. For the last eight years, I have managed teams of people to solve complex problems together. My hope is to continue to lead teams to build amazing things that make people's lives better.
